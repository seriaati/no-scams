## 1. Description of service

no-scams is an automated moderation tool that detects and removes coordinated spam/scam
messages in Discord servers. When a user sends 3 messages across different channels within
2 minutes that are identical in text, share the same image(s), or are all image-only, the
bot deletes those messages and times the user out for 15 minutes.
